Overview

Enhance your business efficiency with our **QR Code Generator and Scanner** module for Odoo. Easily generate, print, and scan QR codes to streamline inventory, sales, and asset management processes.

This module allows you to:
  • Generate unique QR codes for products, invoices, and documents.
  • Scan QR codes using a mobile device or scanner for quick access.
  • Print QR codes directly from Odoo with customizable templates.
  • Integrate QR code scanning into different workflows.
Simplify your operations and reduce manual entry errors with automated QR code solutions.

Features

Instant QR Code Generation: Create QR codes for products, invoices, and customer records with a single click.
Seamless QR Code Scanning: Scan QR codes to retrieve product details, orders, and transactions instantly.
Customizable QR Code Printing: Print QR codes with company branding and adjustable formats.
Integration with Odoo Modules: Works seamlessly with Inventory, Sales, and Accounting modules for improved efficiency.
